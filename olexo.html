BMW X3

The 2025 BMW X3 combines sporty performance with everyday practicality, featuring a refreshed exterior design, advanced technology, and improved fuel efficiency.

With both internal combustion and hybrid options, it caters to a wide range of drivers.

Inside, the X3 is more luxurious than ever, offering a digital curved display and BMW’s latest iDrive 9 system for enhanced connectivity and control. It’s a perfect balance of comfort, capability, and innovation.

  • Advanced Infotainment: Curved display with BMW iDrive 9 and improved voice controls.
  • Powertrains: Turbocharged petrol and plug-in hybrid options for better performance and efficiency.
  • Interior Comfort: Premium materials, ambient lighting, and spacious seating.
  • Safety & Assistance: Upgraded driver-assistance features like adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assist.
  • Driving Dynamics: Sporty handling with all-wheel drive options and fine-tuned suspension

MG Cyberster

The MG Cyberster is an all-electric roadster from MG (Morris Garages), marking the brand’s return to its sporty, two-seater roots — but with a modern, electric twist.

Designed in the UK and built in China under SAIC Motor, the Cyberster blends retro-inspired styling with futuristic performance.

  • Body Style: 2-door electric convertible (roadster)
  • Design: Sleek, low-slung body with scissor (Lambo-style) doors and LED light signatures

porsche 9113.7

The 2025 Porsche 911 (992.2 generation) features a lightly refreshed design, refined performance, and the introduction of a hybrid powertrain on the GTS model.

The base Carrera retains the rear/flat‑six, twin‑turbo engine, now slightly more potent, while Turbo and Turbo S models continue to utilize a hearty 3.7 L flat‑six.

Across the range, expect sharper aerodynamics, modern Matrix‑LED lighting, updated bumpers, and a fully digital instrument display.

  • Engine: 3.7‑liter twin‑turbo flat‑six
    • Produces 572 hp in Turbo and 640 hp in Turbo S trim
    • Turbo S torque reaches 590 lb-ft
  • Acceleration & Performance
    • 0–60 mph in approximately 2.7–2.8 seconds (Turbo), ~2.3–2.6 s for Turbo S variants
    • Top speed up to 205 mph (330 km/h)
  • Drivetrain & Transmission
    • Standard all‑wheel drive (AWD)
    • 8‑speed PDK dual‑clutch automatic
